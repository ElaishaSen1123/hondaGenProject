1970s-1980s: The Early Years

The birth of the Honda Civic—an economical and reliable car for the world.

1970s-1980s Facts

  • First introduced in 1972.
  • One of the first cars with CVCC technology.
  • Compact, fuel-efficient, and reliable.
Max Speed: 100 mph (160 km/h)

1990s-2000s: Innovation & Performance

The Civic evolved into a performance icon while remaining a practical car.

1990s-2000s Facts

  • The Civic Type R was introduced.
  • Became a favorite for street racing.
  • Better aerodynamics and VTEC engines.
Max Speed: 145 mph (233 km/h)

2010s-2020s: The Modern Era

The Civic leads in technology, safety, and hybrid innovation.

2010s-2020s Facts

  • More focus on hybrid technology.
  • First turbocharged Civic.
  • Gained modern safety features.
Max Speed: 180 mph (290 km/h)
